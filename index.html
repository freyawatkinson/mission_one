// About


I am a Freelancer specialising in Adventure Travel. I have spent my whole life with an intense passion to learn, and this was a natural role for me as I spend my time at work constantly discovering the world around me.

An expert in the Adventure Travel industry, I have experience across the board (and the world) ranging from operations to media production, as a writer and travel administrator to tour leader.

With each role I have taken on new skills and experience, not just practical but also personal. After all, smashing a project deadline is much less stressful after hiking solo across Afghanistan.




// What I do


I am currently working as an Intern with the Adventure Travel Trade Association (ATTA), the largest association within the adventure travel space connecting operators, tourism boards and suppliers around the world. This is in partnership with Tomorrow's Air - A ground-breaking project in collaboration with Climeworks that aims to fund the removal and storage of carbon from the atmosphere, providing cleaner air while educating and inspiring.

Personally, my achievements include solo travel and expeditions to over 70 countries, crossing the world by train and a solo hiking expedition to the Pamir Mountains in Afghanistan.

I am currently training in Full Stack Development, a by-product of closed borders and a few lockdowns that has turned into a new passion leading to a unique skill combination of adventure travel/technology. My current specializations include HTML, CSS and JavaScipt with a focus on Front End Development.
